How to Use CalcPro

Learn how to make the most of our advanced calculator toolkit

1

Choose Your Calculator

Start by selecting the calculator you need from the home page or navigation menu. We offer five specialized calculators:

  • Basic Calculator: For simple arithmetic operations
  • Scientific Calculator: For advanced mathematical functions
  • Age Calculator: To calculate exact age in years, months, and days
  • BMI Calculator: For Body Mass Index calculations
  • Interest Calculator: For simple and compound interest calculations
2

Enter Your Values

Depending on the calculator, enter the required values:

Basic Calculator:

Click the number buttons or use your keyboard to enter values

Scientific Calculator:

Use function buttons for advanced operations (sin, cos, log, etc.)

Age Calculator:

Select your birth date and optional target date

BMI Calculator:

Enter weight and height in your preferred units

Interest Calculator:

Input principal amount, interest rate, and time period

3

Perform Calculations

Execute the calculation based on the calculator type:

Basic Operations:

  • Click operation buttons (+, -, ×, ÷)
  • Press equals (=) to get result
  • Use clear (C) to reset

Form-based Calculators:

  • Click "Calculate" button
  • View detailed results
  • See visual representations
4

Customize Your Experience

Personalize CalcPro to suit your preferences:

Theme Toggle:

Switch between dark and light modes using the theme button in the bottom-right corner

Unit Selection:

Choose between metric and imperial units in BMI calculator

Interest Types:

Switch between simple and compound interest calculations
