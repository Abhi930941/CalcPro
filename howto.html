Getting Started Guide

Follow these simple steps to become a CalcPro expert

1

Choose Your Calculator

Start by selecting the calculator that best fits your needs from our comprehensive suite:

  • Basic Calculator: For everyday arithmetic operations
  • Scientific Calculator: For advanced mathematical functions
  • Age Calculator: To calculate exact age in years, months, and days
  • BMI Calculator: For health and fitness calculations
  • Interest Calculator: For financial planning and investments
2

Enter Your Values

Each calculator has its own input method. Here's how to enter values correctly:

  • Basic & Scientific: Click buttons or use keyboard shortcuts
  • Age Calculator: Select dates using date picker
  • BMI Calculator: Enter weight and height in preferred units
  • Interest Calculator: Input principal, rate, and time period
  • All Calculators: Use Tab key to navigate between fields
3

Perform Calculations

Execute calculations based on the calculator type. Here are the methods:

  • Basic Calculator: Click operation buttons or press Enter
  • Scientific Calculator: Use function buttons for advanced operations
  • Form Calculators: Click "Calculate" button to get results
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Use Enter for calculate, Escape for clear
  • Error Handling: Invalid inputs show helpful error messages
4

Customize Your Experience

Personalize CalcPro to match your preferences and workflow:

  • Theme Toggle: Switch between dark and light modes
  • Unit Selection: Choose metric or imperial units in BMI calculator
  • Interest Types: Switch between simple and compound interest
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard support for faster operation
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on all device sizes

+
